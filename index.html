About

In 2020 the Men’s NCAA Division I postseason tournament was cancelled due to Covid-19. Although the teams played a full season, they had to forgo the “March Madness” National Tournament. Based on regular season team metrics this application shows our predictions for the 2020 NCAA National Basketball Tournament. The winners, losers, and Cinderella’s (the underdog’s who performed well despite being ranked low).
